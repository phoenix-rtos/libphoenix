fabs
~~~~

.. c:autodoc:: mathd/fabsd.c

Special cases
^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| x                        | Result                   |
+==========================+==========================+
| :math:`±0`               | :math:`+0`               |
+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| :math:`±Inf`             | :math:`+Inf`             |
+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| :math:`qNaN`             | :math:`qNaN`             |
+--------------------------+--------------------------+
| :math:`sNaN`             | :math:`sNaN`             |
+--------------------------+--------------------------+

Mathematical Approach
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The approach is to mask off the signbit of :math:`x` and return :math:`x`.
